Tikal, in Flores, Petén

Discover the awe-inspiring ancient wonders of Tikal! Immerse yourself in the mystical aura of Mayan ruins, where towering pyramids and lush jungle converge. Unearth history, explore grand plazas, and witness the beauty of a civilization lost in time. Your adventure awaits in the heart of Guatemala's rainforest.

Semuc Champey, in Cobán, Alta Verapaz

Escape to the hidden gem of Guatemala - Semuc Champey! Immerse yourself in crystal-clear turquoise pools, carved naturally by the Cahabón River. Hike through lush rainforests, explore limestone caves, and embrace the breathtaking beauty of this paradise. Adventure and serenity await in the heart of Central America

Lake Atitlan, in Sololá

Discover the magic of Lake Atitlan in Guatemala! Nestled among towering volcanoes, this pristine lake is a true natural wonder. Immerse yourself in the vibrant indigenous culture of lakeside villages, explore lush coffee plantations, and witness some of the world's most stunning sunsets. Your journey to serenity and adventure begins here.

How much budget do I need to travel to Guatemala?

Explore Guatemala without breaking the bank! This Central American gem offers incredible experiences at affordable prices. From budget-friendly accommodations to delicious street food, you can stretch your dollars while immersing yourself in rich culture, stunning landscapes, and ancient history. Guatemala awaits your adventure without the budget worries.

Why travel to Guatemala?

Guatemala, where adventure meets authenticity! Immerse yourself in the vibrant tapestry of Mayan culture, explore ancient ruins, savor mouthwatering cuisine, and trek through diverse landscapes – from lush rainforests to towering volcanoes. Guatemala offers a unique blend of history, nature, and warmth from its people. Don't just travel; experience the heart and soul of a country waiting to captivate your senses. ?

What is it that most attracts the attention of tourists in Guatemala?

What captivates travelers in Guatemala? It's the mesmerizing blend of ancient ruins like Tikal, the stunning beauty of Lake Atitlan, vibrant indigenous cultures, and the warmth of its people. From colorful markets to breathtaking landscapes, Guatemala is a sensory feast waiting to be explored. Dive into this world of wonders and let Guatemala steal your heart.
